HeliotropeTrigger, with DO Architecture, Hanna Tuulikki, John Eagles, Stefanie Posavec, Molly Naylor, and Justin Quillinan

Heliotrope was an immersive installation that explored our relationship with light. Existing in both physical and virtual spaces, it highlighted the impact of light on our health and investigate Seasonal Affective Disorder.

Trigger worked with a collaborative team of artists, designers and scientists including DO Architecture, sound artist Hanna Tuulikki, psychiatrist John Eagles, designer Stefanie Posavec, writer Molly Naylor, and developer Justin Quillinan.
